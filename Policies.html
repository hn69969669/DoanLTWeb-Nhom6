POLICIES
1. General Policies
1.1 Property Listings
All property listings are regularly updated to reflect current availability. However, due to high demand, availability may change quickly. We encourage you to inquire about specific properties to confirm their status.
1.2 Booking & Reservations
  • • Reservations can be made through our website or by contacting our sales team.
  • • A reservation is not confirmed until a deposit or payment is received.
  • • All bookings are subject to availability and confirmation from our team.
1.3 Cancellation & Refunds
  • • Cancellations must be made at least 48 hours in advance of the scheduled viewing or booking to receive a full refund.
  • • Cancellations made within 48 hours are subject to a 20% cancellation fee.
  • • Refunds will be processed within 7 business days after cancellation.
1.4 Property Viewing
  • • Property viewings are scheduled upon request, based on availability.
  • • Please arrive on time for your scheduled viewing to ensure a smooth experience.
  • • Our agents will provide a guided tour and answer any questions you may have about the property.
2. Payment Policies
2.1 Payment Methods
We accept the following payment methods:
  • • Bank Transfer
  • • Credit/Debit Cards
  • • Cash (in-person only)
2.2 Payment Terms
  • • Full payment is required upon signing the lease or purchase agreement unless otherwise stated.
  • • If a payment plan is available, the terms and schedules will be clearly stated in the contract.
2.3 Late Payments
  • • A late fee of 5% of the total amount due will be charged for payments made after the due date.
  • • Failure to settle payments within 30 days may result in the cancellation of the booking or property lease.
Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
3.1 How can I schedule a property viewing?
All property listings are regularly updated to reflect current availability. However, due to high demand, availability may change quickly. We encourage you to inquire about specific properties to confirm their status.
3.2 Are your properties available for rent or only for sale?
We offer both rental and sale properties. Each listing specifies whether it is for rent or for sale.
3.3 Can I negotiate the price of the property?
Property prices are set based on current market values. However, our agents may offer flexibility in certain circumstances, particularly for long-term leases or bulk purchases.
3.4 What should I bring to the viewing?
For property viewings, you should bring a valid ID and any relevant financial documentation if you're interested in renting or purchasing. This includes proof of income or a credit report.
3.5 Do you offer financing options?
We work with several trusted financial institutions to offer financing options. If you're interested in financing, please reach out to our team to discuss available solutions.
3.6 How long does it take to process a purchase or rental agreement?
The processing time for a rental or purchase agreement typically takes between 7-10 business days, depending on the complexity of the transaction and the availability of required documents.
3.7 What happens if I need to cancel my reservation?
If you need to cancel a reservation, please notify us at least 48 hours in advance for a full refund. Cancellations made within 48 hours will incur a cancellation fee.
3.8 Are pets allowed in rental properties?
Pet policies vary by property. Please refer to the individual listing or consult with our team to determine the pet policy for a specific property.
3.9 What happens if there is a maintenance issue?
If you encounter a maintenance issue, please contact our customer support team immediately. We’ll arrange for the necessary repairs within 48 hours for most urgent issues.
